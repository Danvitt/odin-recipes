Chicken Fajitas

Sizzling Fajitas Restaruant style

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • bell peppers
  • Onion
  • fajita seasoning
  • vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Add a ½ cup of water and ½ cup of apple cider vinegar to the instant pot. Add 3 tablespoons of liquid smoke.

  2. Step 2:

    3. cut frozen ribs into 3 or 4 rib sections and place in the pot on a basket trivet.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Set instant pot for to cook high pressure for 1 hour.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 15 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid.

  5. Step 4:

    6. Preheat the oven broiler. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source.

  6. Step 5:

    7. Transfer ribs to a rimmed baking sheet. Brush with barbecue sauce. Broil until glazed, 4 to 5 minutes.
