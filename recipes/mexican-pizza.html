Mexican Pizza

This Mexican Pizza will ring Tacos Bell!

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • 1lb ground hamburger
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1 can refried beans
  • black olives sliced
  • pepper jack cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • tomatoes
  • sour cream
  • olive oil
  • green onion
  • your favorite taco seasoning
  • 1 onion

Directions

  1. Step 1: Prepare the tortillas

    2. heat vegetable oil in a pan to the point that it sizzles when sprinkled with a drop of water. Adding a teaspoon of cumin to the oil will give a taste of authentic taco flavor. Next, place the tortilla in the pan to brown it. Poking some pin holes in the tortillas will help prevent bubbles from forming in them. Once browned set aside and repeat the process for all the tortillas. Set the tortillas to the side

  2. Step 2: brown the meat

    3. Add 1lb of ground of beef to a frying pan and chopped it up with your spatula. Add 1 cup of chopped onions. When the meat is no longer pink, add your taco mix and a half cup of water. Bring the water to a boil and then let simmer.

  3. Step 3: Assembly

    4. For the first layer, place a torilla on a nonstick baking pan, spread refried beans, add hamburger mixture and then drizzle enchlada sauce. Add shredded cheddar cheese.

    For the top layer add your second torilla, pepperjack cheese, black olives, chopped tomatoes and green onions.

  4. Step 4: Heat in the oven

    5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Once preheatd place pan in the oven for approximately 10mins or until cheese has melted.

  5. Step 5: Enjoy
