Fall Off the Bone Ribs

This Mexican Pizza will ring Tacos Bell!

Ingredients

  • 1 rack baby back ribs
  • ½ cup favorite bbq sauce
  • black Pepper
  • ½ apple juice or water
  • 3 tablespoons hickory smoke seasoning
  • black Pepper
  • salt

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Pour water into a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot). Set the trivet inside.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Sprinkle hickory smoke seasoning liberally over frozen ribs; set ribs on top of the trivet. Close and lock the lid.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set the timer for 45 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 15 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid.

  5. Step 3:

    6. Preheat the oven broiler. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source.

  6. Step 4:

    7. Transfer ribs to a rimmed baking sheet. Brush with barbecue sauce. Broil until glazed, 4 to 5 minutes.
